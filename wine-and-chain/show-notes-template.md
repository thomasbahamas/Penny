## SEO TIPS FOR SHOW NOTES

### Title Format
`Episode [#]: [Catchy Title] ‚Äî Wine and Chain`  
Example: `Episode 3: Tokenizing the Vineyard ‚Äî Wine and Chain`

### First 150 Characters Matter
The first paragraph becomes the meta description. Make it compelling.

### Use Headers (H2, H3)
- Google loves structure
- Makes notes skimmable
- Helps with accessibility

### Include Timestamps (Optional but Good)
```
## Timestamps
- 0:00 ‚Äî Intro + What's in the Glass
- 5:30 ‚Äî Crypto Corner: RWA Tokenization
- 22:15 ‚Äî Guest Interview with [Name]
- 45:00 ‚Äî Outro + Next Week Preview
```

### Link to Previous Episodes
Build internal links ‚Äî helps SEO and keeps listeners in your content.

### Add Transcript
- Huge SEO boost (Google indexes all that text)
- Accessibility win
- Descript generates this automatically
