## PRE-SHOW CHECKLIST (30 min before)

### Tech
- [ ] Riverside session created & link sent to Jill
- [ ] Audio levels checked (both mics, -12dB to -6dB target)
- [ ] Camera framing checked (rule of thirds, eye level)
- [ ] Lighting balanced (no harsh shadows, warm tone)
- [ ] Background clean (wine bottles visible? Cambria branding?)
- [ ] Internet speed test (5+ Mbps upload minimum)
- [ ] Backup recording started (local audio backup)

### Wine Prep
- [ ] Bottle selected & opened
- [ ] Wine breathing (15-30 min before show)
- [ ] Glasses ready (2 clean wine glasses)
- [ ] Spit bucket nearby (if needed for long episodes)
- [ ] Water for palate cleansing

### Content Prep
- [ ] Crypto topics outlined (1-2 topics max)
- [ ] Key stats/facts bookmarked
- [ ] Any links to share copied to clipboard
- [ ] Guest briefed (if applicable)

## SHOW SEGMENTS

### SEGMENT 1: INTRO (3-5 min)
- Welcome viewers
- "What's in the Glass" — Jill introduces the wine
- Quick crypto market temperature check (Torrey)
- Housekeeping: subscribe, follow, next week's wine tease

### SEGMENT 2: WINE DEEP DIVE (15-20 min)
- Jill's expertise: varietal, region, producer story
- Tasting notes live
- Food pairings
- "Why this wine matters" angle

### SEGMENT 3: CRYPTO CORNER (15-20 min)
- Torrey's 1-2 degen topics
- Current events, tokenization, RWA, Solana ecosystem
- Keep it accessible — explain jargon for wine people
- Connect back to wine when possible (tokenization, provenance, etc.)

### SEGMENT 4: CORK TALK / GUEST (10-15 min)
- Community questions from chat
- OR guest interview
- OR special segment (wine + crypto news roundup)

### SEGMENT 5: OUTRO (3-5 min)
- Final thoughts on the wine
- Next week's wine preview
- Call to action: subscribe, review, follow on X
- "See you next Wine Wednesday"

- [ ] Export clean audio (MP3, 128kbps)

### Publishing

- [ ] Upload video to YouTube
